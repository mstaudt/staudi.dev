Dino World Run now available on iOS

Posted on 21. April 2025

#Dino World Run

Great news for all Apple users: Dino World Run is now available on the App Store!

After the Android release in late March, the game can now be played on iPhone and iPad as well.

I’m super excited to bring the game to even more players. Just like the Android version, this one was built with Godot and all graphics were hand-drawn in ProCreate on my iPad – a real passion project from start to launch.

Give it a try and let me know what you think – I’d love to hear your feedback!

In the coming weeks, a larger update is coming to the game that will add more levels and accessories.
