Handelsblatt awards spritpreise.jetzt as best online portal

Posted on 10. September 2025

#Spritpreise #Award

Spritpreise.jetzt was awarded best online portal of 2025 in the fuel cost comparison category by Handelsblatt, a leading German newspaper focusing on business and finance.

This makes me incredibly proud, as it shows that the mission to make the fuel market more transparent is hitting the mark. Handelsblatt put the portals through their paces - from user-friendliness to added value for users.

You can read the entire Handelsblatt article here:
