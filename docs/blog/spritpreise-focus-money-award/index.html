Focus Money awards spritpreise.jetzt

Posted on 19. July 2025

#Spritpreise #Award

We are delighted to announce that spritpreise.jetzt has received an award in the current issue of Focus Money magazine (issue 30/2025)!

In the comprehensive study “Best Online Portals”, which was carried out by the analysis and consulting institute Service Value on behalf of Focus Money, users gave us a top rating in the “Fuel Cost Comparison” category.

This award means a great deal to us, as it is based directly on the opinions of over 94,000 consumers. Only portals that perform better than the overall average of all providers assessed within their sector receive a “top score” award.

This is a fantastic confirmation of our work and a huge thank you to you, our users! Your trust and satisfaction are our daily incentive to offer the best and most transparent service for comparing fuel prices.

We will continue to do everything we can to help you save at the gas station.
