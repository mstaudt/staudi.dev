Spritpreise: Price alarm now available

Posted on 24. June 2025

#Spritpreise

Spritpreise has received a useful new feature – the Price Alarm! Starting now, you can set a personal price threshold for your favorite gas stations. If the price for Super E5, E10, or Diesel drops below this value, you’ll automatically receive a push notification directly to your smartphone.

You’re on vacation or have recently filled up? No worries, pause the price alarm with one simple click for a few hours or days - until you need it again.

The update will be rolled out to all users in the coming days. Try it out and feel free to give some feedback!

If you’re interested in some technical details, keep an eye on the upcoming blog posts.
