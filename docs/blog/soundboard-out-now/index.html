New project: Soundboard app

Posted on 17. March 2026

#Soundboard

I am excited to introduce a very special new project today: the Soundboard App.

This time, it is not just another utility application, but a joint development project realized together with a friend’s daughter. The goal was to bring her creative ideas to life and build a fully functional application from scratch together.

From a technical perspective, the Android app was built entirely using Jetpack Compose. It features a modern, clean interface. All the built-in sound effects included in the app were either recorded by ourselves or sourced from freesound.org - a great collaborative database for Creative Commons licensed sounds.

Feel free to check out the app!
