Data collected automatically by us

We automatically collect data when you use one of our apps. This information includes aggregated user data (which does not allow us to identify you) as described in more detail below:

When you use one of our apps, we automatically receive certain information about your mobile device and usage of the app, such as operating system, version and other similar information such as time spent in different parts of the app and error rates, among other things.

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements within the app. Google may use the advertising ID of your end device, as well as cookies and/or similar technologies, to collect personal data for the purpose of generating and displaying personalized advertising. You can find more detailed information about what data Google collects and how it is processed here: https://www.google.com/policies/technologies/ads. Google’s full privacy policy can be found here: https://www.google.com/policies/privacy/
